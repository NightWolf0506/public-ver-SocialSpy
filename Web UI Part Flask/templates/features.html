FEATURES

  • Selective Screenshot Based Report:

    Automatically captures and documents key sections of a social media account, including posts, messages, and profiles, generating comprehensive reports with screenshots for investigation.

  • OCR Filtering for Posts and Messages:

    Utilizes Optical Character Recognition (OCR) technology to extract text from images (e.g., screenshots of posts or messages), ensuring that all content, including embedded text, is captured for analysis.

  • Automatic Summary Generation:

    Automatically compiles a summary of extracted social media data, highlighting key activities such as frequent contacts, message volumes, and significant posts, for quick and efficient analysis.

  • Search for a Person of Interest:

    Allows investigators to search for mentions of specific individuals (e.g., tagged users or frequent contacts) within the parsed social media data, aiding in connecting suspects to key individuals.

  • Multi-Social Platform Automation at Same Time:

    Enables simultaneous data extraction from multiple social media platforms (e.g., Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, WhatsApp, etc.), streamlining the investigative process across various channels.

  • Frequent Contacted Friend Posts Capture:

    Analyzes message data to identify frequently contacted individuals or recurring names in posts, helping investigators trace relationships and connections between suspects and others.
